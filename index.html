The Corporate Ladder

  • This was created for a event in Spartasoft (Game Development club at Michigan State University).
  • Players play as a businessperson ascending a building floor by floor by stacking office supplies and coworkers on top of each other.
  • I worked primarily on the programming portion of the project, in particular I created:
    • The player's movement system.
    • A pick up/throw mechanic used to attack objects and enemies
    • The health and damage system for the player.
